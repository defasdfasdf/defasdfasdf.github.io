How to get SHIELD?
  1. The only way to gain SHIELD by buying is by buying an other currency first, what currencies are supported are listed on the mainpage underneeth "Exchanges".
  2. After you have found a supported currency, you'll have to send it to one of the supported exchanges (the transaction can take up to several hours).
  3. Once you have chosen an exchange you'll have to register on it. We also recommend enabling 2FA Authentication, which will make your account more secure.
  4. If your registration was successful, are you now able to set an order on the exchange. If you set a buy order and someone sells it for the same price you will receive your coins.
  5. (This step is optional) Your coins are now in the wallet of the exchange to export them to another wallet you'll need a deposit address to send it to. Click on the withdraw button and enter the deposit address of your deposit address. Transfering the SHIELD should only take several minutes.
  6. Congratulations, you have successfully bought SHIELD, if you followed all the previous steps!
